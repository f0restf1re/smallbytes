Hey there! We are Project Small Bytes - a CE01 project initiated by students from Raffles Institution, in partnership with YMCA Singapore and 1010Media.

Our project aims to equip underprivileged students with elementary programming skills, including Python and data visualisation.

We are currently recruiting volunteers from RI and reaching out to young students who could benefit from our programme.
